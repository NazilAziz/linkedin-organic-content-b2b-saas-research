## Why selected

Chris Walker pioneered the **"dark social"** narrative in B2B — the idea that most pipeline influence happens in channels attribution tools undercount, especially LinkedIn DMs, Slack shares, and word-of-mouth. For B2B SaaS organic strategy, this reframes LinkedIn from a top-of-funnel vanity channel to a **primary revenue influence layer**.

He advocates:
- Founder and executive-led LinkedIn over generic company pages
- Demand creation vs. demand capture balance
- Skepticism of MQL-centric reporting for social content
- Podcast-led and ungated content as trust builders

Walker is selected because he connects LinkedIn organic activity directly to **pipeline economics** — the language SaaS leadership cares about.
