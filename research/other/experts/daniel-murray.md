## Why selected

Daniel Murray built **The Marketing Millennials** into one of the largest B2B marketing media brands on LinkedIn. He demonstrates how consistent, personality-driven content can grow a massive professional audience and convert it into events, sponsorships, and community — a playbook SaaS companies can adapt at smaller scale.

He models:
- Relatable, meme-aware B2B content that still respects the audience
- Media-brand building on LinkedIn (not just individual thought leadership)
- Interview and social clip distribution from podcast content
- Community and event flywheels fueled by organic reach

Murray is selected for **volume content ops and B2B-native entertainment** — how to stay visible in crowded SaaS feeds without being boring.
