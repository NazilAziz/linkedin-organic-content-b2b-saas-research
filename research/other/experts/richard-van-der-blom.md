## Why selected

Richard van der Blom is the leading **independent researcher of the LinkedIn algorithm and B2B engagement patterns**. His annual *Algorithm Insights* report is the most widely cited data source for what actually works on LinkedIn — critical grounding for any B2B SaaS organic strategy that would otherwise rely on anecdotes.

He provides:
- Large-sample analysis of post formats, timing, and engagement drivers
- Evidence on document posts, polls, video, and text-only performance
- Guidance on profile optimization and connection strategy
- Regular updates as LinkedIn shifts distribution rules

Van der Blom is selected because **strategy must follow the algorithm's constraints** — especially for SaaS teams investing executive time in posting.
