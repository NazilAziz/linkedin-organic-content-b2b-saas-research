## Why selected

Dave Gerhardt is a defining voice in **modern B2B marketing brand-building**. As former CMO of Drift and founder of Exit Five, he champions conversational, human marketing over traditional demand-gen playbooks — directly relevant to how SaaS companies should sound on LinkedIn.

He models:
- Strong point-of-view posts that attract a specific ICP
- Community-led distribution (Exit Five as content amplifier)
- Clear, jargon-free copy that reads like a person, not a press release
- Founder/CMO-as-media strategy for category creation

For B2B SaaS organic LinkedIn, Gerhardt is the benchmark for **tone, positioning, and building an audience before you need pipeline**.
